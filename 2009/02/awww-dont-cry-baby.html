Monday, February 02, 2009

awww... dont cry baby


How can you not melt at this sight?
I teared a little seeing Roger Federer cry on the podium in Rod Laver Arena
Not because the man is cute and successful and i feel sad for him
Imagine being at the top for so long and suddenly this Spanish Muchacho comes along and is beating you in all the matches and you just can figure out why.
I guess it you can only feel the same pain only if you've been at the top
Ive been glued to my TV since 5pm all the way till almost 10pm catching the finals of the Australian Open, screaming my lungs out
to be honest, I was really hopeful and somehow sure that Federer was gonna win this one and bring it back home, but he gave a good fight i must say.
Im just upset that a legend and true champion is tested like that
The australian open is definitely a very special event for Federer as this was where he got his No. 1 title while playing in 04 i think.
Losing at the same place must have sucked big time.
I never thought i'd say this but Roger looks really cute crying.
Its somehow different from his nonchalant, calm face he wears on court during his match.
Not that i wanna see him cry anymore
The next one will be a Paris, Roland Garros which is Nadal's niche terrain, the clay courts
Roger, you go to Paris and steal that damn cup away from that man i tell you.
Make Nadal cry instead
Oh gawd, i look forward to the day he gets back his no. 1 and wins another Grandslam to even Pete Sampras' record.
Good luck baby
Till then, i'll always be screming infront of the TV for you
p.s: im having serious post australian opens withdrawal syndrome now. I'm feeling so empty with nothing to look forward to anymore.
