guy that are waiting to date me except for the last one.

remember me telling you guys that ive been watching a lot of movies on my computer lately?
well, i decided to make a list of my favourite male leads cause well, im bored and i would like to know who are your faavourite male leads as well??
so feel free to comment on this post.

first of all, who doesnt like a hero?
from my list of my favourite males, i realised they all potray the character of some sort of a hero, making me all toasty in my heart
Dont judge me now, im know im the only one saying this out loud.
But dont you just wished you were the female lead in the movie sometimes??
hahah

1. Character: Edward Cullen
Movie: Twilight
Actor: Robert Pattison


Edward is a VAMPIRE
i know.. who wants a vampire for a boyfriend right?
but in twilight this bunch of vampires are not your typical cape wearing, coffin sleeping vampire
to add on, Edward is like super rich, stays in a mansion and drives a very nice sports car.
I like how he is super intriqued and portective over Bella and is always there to rescue he when she needs him- like miraculouslly appear!!
The thing is abt Edward is that he is a vampire and Bella is a human, so he is caught in this tension because he wants to drink her blood so badly but is in love with her. I just love it when a man knows how to control himself to protect someone he loves
So tell me why dont you like vampires again??

2. Character: Jack Dawson
Movie: Titanic
Actor: Leonardo De Caprio


I know! Yes, i watched Titanic
And more than once.
Jack gave up his life for Rose man.
He taught the poor little rich girl the real meaning of life and lead her out of her misery of the pretencious life she was forced to lead by her mother
Jack was very charming and also super protective over Rose. He is not rich and super poor but he enjoys his life as it is and isnt afraid of any obstacles.
I am loving his survivor spirit and how he lead Rose all the way to the top of the ship when it was about to sink. So smart.
Jack died in the end cause the plank of floating wood was two small for two people and and decided to let Rose have it instead.
How sweet?
I dunno. I just melt everytime i watch the film although its bloody long, i love how their romance just builds up on the very short trip on the ship.
Couple with Leo's good looks, Jack Dawson is on the top of the list for sure!!!

Side tracking, if you decide to watch this movie again, can you please count how many time they say each other's name? Jack and Rose.
Count it.
I dunno if its me, but i think the name calling thing kinda irritates me a little. They call each other's name in every freaking sentence.
Other than that, awesome movie.

3. Character: Achilles
Movie: Troy
Actor: Brad Pitt


Set in anciet grecian time, Achilles was know to be a very merciless warrior, killing every man that gets in his way without much trouble.
But in the movie, he actually died while trying to save Briseis the daughter of his supposed "enemy" which he was supposed to kill.
got shot in heel by an arrow.
I love this character becasue he was supposed to be the bad guy, but deep down he was actualy a good person, and he showed it by giving up his life to save the woman he loves.
That's like the epitome of sweetness
He was very suave also, as he didnt give a hoot about the king he serves. He was just a warrior at heart and hence he kills whoever he wishes to kill.
Why do all the good and cute guys have to die in the movies???

4. Character: Danny Archer
Movie: Blood Diamonds
Actor: Leonardo De Caprio


Its Leo again. I have to say he is my favourite actor of all time because of his ability to make characters come to life with so much convition.
Danny Archer is the bad guy.
He smuggles blood diamonds of Sierra Leon to amercian diamond merchants in the black market.
He finds a rare pink diamond and tries to get hold of it so that he can have a better life and get out of South Africa. He meets this american journalist who is super anti blood diamonds and is here to do a story on smuggling diamonds
Obviouslly they are at loggerheads, but in the end, he once again, sacrificed his life so that she could get her story and also helped a poor african man and his family break free from the choas of Sierra Leon.
Like Jack, Danny is super resourceful and is a great risk taker.
Super charming.
I think i just like Leonardo.
He acted really well in this movie with the South African accent and all going on. He was super convincing which is why i think he is one of the best actor of all times.

4. Character: Bailian of Ibelin
Movie: Kingdom of Heaven
Actor: Orlando Bloom


Bailian is the son of some favourite knight of the king of Jeruselem whom he died a while after acknowledging him as he took an arrow for Bailian in a fight.
Bailian was knighted by his father as soon he found himself takng over the job of his dad, being the king's right hand man.
The king's brother in law was the next in line to ascend the throne after the sickly king who was going to die of leoprosy soon.
The thing about him is that he was such a nuisance he kept wegding a war with the muslims whom the king has spent many years to maintain a peaceful relationship with in Jeruselem.
He killed the sister of Saladin who was the muslim king. And the war between the Christians and Muslims starts.
The muslim had many solidier.
Through the show you can see how Bailian prepared the city for war.
When there wasnt enough knights, he mass knighted every man in the city giving them alot of confidence.
He surrended in the end to protect the lives of his people in exchange for Jeruselem.
Saladin promised him that every christian will be safely moved out of Jeruselem.
Tale of a brave and wonderful knight. Loves.

5. Character: Sam Dawson
Movie: I am Sam
Actor: Sean Penn


Sam is a little mentally retarded, he works at Starbucks and has a daughter named Lucy Diamonds after his favourite band Beatle's song.
As lucy grows older, he found that his limitations is holding lucy back
Lucy on the other hand tries to appear not too smart infront of Sam, like reading Green eggs and ham everyday instead of a higher level Stellaluna as she knows he is "different from other dads"
The authorities soon found abt them and took Lucy away from Sam and put her in foster care.
Lucy often escapes back home just to be with Sam. The foster parents couldnt take it and wants to adopt lucy for good.
Sam found this lawyer to help him who was having her own family problems.
Together while fighting the case, Rita learns many things about true love from Sam- the last person she would think of.
What i find fascinating about Sam is that he is very simple minded, yet he makes a lot of sense all the time.
Vey touching movie about the love a father and daughter share.
Especially when the father is so simple, he just loves his daughter and people all around him for who they are.
Never judging them.
I wished more people like that exist in my world.

that concludes my list of male leads.
Please take time to wacth these films if you haven't
They are all very nice shows with alot of meaning.
Maybe except for twilight and titanic.
But just watch them for the handsome leonardo de caprio will you???
Leonardo is officially the new roger federer for now!!!
