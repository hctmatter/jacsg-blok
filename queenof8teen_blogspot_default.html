Hello from me and baby!

It's been soooo long since I logged on to blog here. Just a short entry this evening and guess what, I'm blogging from my new macbook pro! :D

A flashback of 2010
Friday, December 31 10:49 PM
Okay, I kept to my promise. I told ya I'll be back before the year ends, which is like, in an hour. While I'm waiting for the countdown show to start at 11pm, I thought of posting a short entry to sum up my entire 2010.

In one word, it has been AMAZING!

January



Renovation for our 1st home was in progress. Can't believe that was almost a year ago. Now, we're enjoying ourselves in this nest we built on our own. Hubs and I also celebrated the last CNY that we can officially collect red packets. Hee.

February

Received my long awaited emplacement and celebrated our 4th Valentine's Day. :)

March



Had our wedding photoshoot. Super fun, super tired but it was all worth it. The memory, we will keep close to our hearts for a long long time to come. 3-month countdown to the big day.

April



Got baptised during Easter. Spent my last month with MOF after a good 3 years. Bade farewell to the place but not the many friends I've made during my time there. Thankful for the opportunity given!

May


Started work at CCS. Fabulous place to work at, loving the group of colleagues who have became amazing friends to me over the short course of 8 months.

June


Became Mrs Lee to the bestest husband ever. Happily ever after began from then. :)

July


Went to Brunei for a work trip. First time to Brunei, interesting experience.

August

Singapore turned a year older.

September
Turned a year older. At 24, it is slightly more than 2 decades and considering all I've accomplished and been through, it has been a good 24 years. I thank my parents for bringing me to this world 24 years old.

Also went for our honeymoon in Korea! Wheeee!

October

Passed too quickly!

November

Passed too quickly!

December

Attended the Catechists training session at SJI to get ready for our duty next year. Hubs and I also celebrated our first Christmas together! At the same time, we're working towards building our own family!

I'll get to my 2011 resolutions in a while. For now, happy 2011 to you who have chanced upon my blog. May God's blessings be upon you always.

In the meantime, I wish..

XoXo

Mrs Lee
