It's been almost a year since I last wrote.
If I have to choose a word to describe 2012
that word would be.....
alright?

well, there wasn't any life-changing experience
except for the fact that I started working after studying for 16 years.
Basically, things are routine and mundane.
What else besides following my timetable,
going for impromptu meetings,
staying back for event plannings,
replying endless emails,
taking attendance,
...
the list won't end.

oh yes, random but..
I really feel handicapped without my whistle!

but when it comes, I wished it didn't come.
