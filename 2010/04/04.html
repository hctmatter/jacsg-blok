I was talking to melala about dressing up last week after a day of studying
As a PE teahcer, my fashion fate is sealed to a life of PESS shirts and running shorts

Would you teach PE in spoting attire and then change into something nicer to teach in the classroom?
or would you just remain in your sporting attire the entire day?
On one hand, i would think that in order to preserve the image of a PE teacher, the latter would be more appropritae, but Melala was painting this picture of "the" female PE teacher that can rock both formal and sporting outfits.
