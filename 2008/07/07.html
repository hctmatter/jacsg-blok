pls pls catch this!

flicking through the TV channels always gives me some amazing surprises
this time, it led me to the discovery of this amazing show which was aired previously on starhub channel 55 or 255 sometime last year.

But is back for encore!!
Amazing Surgeon Bong Dal Hee















Yes! A korean drama. One out of the 2 that i caught other than Jewel In the Palace
so i assure you it is damn good.

This Medical drama series tells the story of an aspiring doctor who suffers from a critical heart problem since young but foregoes the danger just to fulfill her passion to safe lives. She begins her residency programme by lying to her mum that she is going overseas to pursue another degree.
At the hospital drama builds up where she faces cases like getting infected by an Aids patient' blood, being attacked by a prison inmate and ofcourse romance as well.

So whats so special you say?
This is not the typical local medical drama where operation procedures are cleverly blocked by the actors.
Expect face on operation scenes, medical terms and lots of blood.

Do you know the tube where they stick it up someone's uretha when they can go to the toilet is called a foley?
Super interesting show.
Pls go catch it. Cause i think this is the 2nd encore already.

anyway, do u fancy the girl on the extreme right looks like Olive, Poppeye's wife?
im just saying man

Oh and if you watch it in the original language that is korean, you will find alot of korean words have similar or exactly the same phonetic sound as chinese
Like heart operation in korean from what i heard is "shim~ zang shou shu~"
kinda funny when you hear it.

Go watch it:)

i chanced upon this mail which kinda hit me right in the face.
im still trying to force myself to be a very gracious person because i feel like i am "above average" on the graciousness scale

Two friends were walking throug the desert
During some point of the journey, they had an arguement and one friend slapped the other one in the face.
The one who got slapped was hurt, but without saying anything, wrote in the sand
"today, my best friend slapped me in the face"

They kept on walking until they found an oasis where they decided to take a bath in
The one who got slapped got stucked in a mire and started drwoning, but they friend saved him
After he recovered, he wrote on a stone
"Today, my best friend saved my life"

The same friend then asked
"After i hurt you, you worte in the sand and now you write on a stone. Why is that so?"

The friend replied
"when someone hurt you, you should write it in sand where the winds of forgiveness can erase it away, but when someone does something good for us, we must engrave it on stone where no wind can ever erase it"
